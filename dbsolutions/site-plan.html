Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to publicize the service that I currently perform, which is to support or systematize companies or businesses so that they have data storage and have it available through the implementation of databases and software development to start or increase the level of business. In addition to providing outsourcing services to companies that wish to improve their technologies or develop new information technology solutions.

Audience

This site is aimed at small businesses that want to systematize their processes that they currently do on paper and provide them with computer solutions so that they can control their information and have software. And for established companies, provide data and software support for their current systems on incidents or update software or databases to recent technologies.
